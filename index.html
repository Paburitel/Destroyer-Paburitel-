As a matter of fact we have a dedicated squad of monkeys, they produce bullets during your navigation. Yes, this sounds crazy but it's true! We've spent 10 millions dollars on this design! There is a drawback though, monkeys can only produce bullets just for one gun. That is why the other gun's ammo supply is limited! But the "Double Gun and here is the daddy" features increased damage

And so we have a deadly ship! But what does the enemy have? You will have to face a huge number of bombs, planes and tanks. Enemy numbers will increase as you move forward.

Now then! Use keys "W", "A", "S", "D" to move your ship around. Use "left mouse" key to shoot from your "Mono Gun exterminator 42" and "SPACE" key to shoot from the "Double Gun and here is the daddy"

It sounds easy, but don't make any mistakes!
